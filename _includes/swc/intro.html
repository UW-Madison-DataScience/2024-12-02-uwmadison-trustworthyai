This lesson aims to teach responsible machine learning practices with a focus on fairness, explainability, reproducibility, and safety. Participants will learn about the interpretability, reproducibility, and/or fairness of the models they build. Participants will discuss how to connect the principles applied in this training to models they want to build for their research. Participants must have experience using Python and a basic understanding of machine learning (e.g., familiar with the concepts like train/test split and cross-validation) and should have trained at least one model in the past.
